The Best Holidays Start Here!

Discover the magic of tranquility at Kingsukh Guest House, nestled amidst the breathtaking charm of Biharinath Hill, Baranti Hill, Susunia, Joychandi Pahar, and the peaceful embrace of Maithon, Panchat, and Baranti Dams.

Enjoy cozy stays, scenic views, mouthwatering local cuisine, and a lush garden retreat that soothes your soul. From peaceful mornings to vibrant evenings, every moment here promises a harmonious blend of nature and comfort.

Ideal for families, couples, or solo travelers — Kingsukh is not just a place to stay, but a story to remember. Come explore the lesser-known gems of Purulia and let our heartfelt hospitality create memories that last.
